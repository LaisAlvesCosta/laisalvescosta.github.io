# About

Hello! My name is **Laís Alves da Costa**.

I am a medalist of **OBMEP (Brazilian Public Schools Mathematics Olympiad)**, 
hold a **Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics Education from the University of São Paulo (USP)**, and I am currently a **Master’s student in Computer Science at USP**.

I come from the **Brazilian public education system**, a background that has deeply shaped 
my academic journey and motivated my interest in education, mathematics, and technology.

During my undergraduate studies, I had the opportunity to participate in an **academic exchange at the Università della Svizzera italiana, in Switzerland**, where I broadened my education in an international academic environment.

## About this blog

This blog was created to share:

- studies and notes on mathematics and computer science;
- experiments with Linux and kernel development;
- technical tutorials and problem-solving guides;
- reflections on education, research, and academic life.

The idea is to use this space as a public learning notebook, where I document discoveries, challenges, and solutions throughout my journey in research and technology. If you would like to talk, exchange ideas, or collaborate, feel free to get in touch.
